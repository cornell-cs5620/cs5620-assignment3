Controls:

w - Move Forward / Zoom in on Focus
a - Straif Left
s - Move Backward / Zoom out from Focus
d - Straif Right
e - Move Up
q - Move Down
m - Bind/Unbind Mouse

right click			  - Canvas Menu
right click on object - Object Menu
right click on light  - Light Menu
right click on camera - Camera Menu

left click and drag   - Manipulator (translate, rotate, scale, fovy, aspect, width, height, near, far)
